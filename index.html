WELCOME TO A B ELECTRIC & AUTOMATION

One of our (A B ELECTRIC & AUTOMATION) main business practices is in the range of Marine Equipment supply and export worldwide. Chittagong is one of the world’s largest shipbreaking yards where demolishing many vessels every month hence, Chittagong has become well known to the world shipping market as a great source of new ship spare parts & used reusable ship machinery & equipment. So, we took the opportunity to enter in ship’s equipment trading business globally and earned entire satisfaction from our worldwide customers by supplying the best quality equipment at a competitive price. We have a vast stock of ship machinery, equipment, and spare parts.
