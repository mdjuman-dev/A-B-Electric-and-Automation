Our Story

Founded in 2010, A B Electric & Automation has grown from a small local electrical service provider to a leading company in industrial automation and electrical solutions. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, efficient, and innovative electrical services to businesses and homeowners alike.

Over the years, we've expanded our expertise to include advanced automation systems, smart home solutions, and industrial control systems. Our team of certified electricians and automation specialists are dedicated to staying at the forefront of technology to deliver the best solutions for our clients.
